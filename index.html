Where it all started

  1. Nintendo 64
  2. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  3. Released November 21, 1998
  4. The console and game itself was meant for my older siblings, but I quickly took ownership!

Truly I became hooked, and at the age of 5, I pulled my first all nighter by sneaking out to the living room and playing

The Years Following

When I was 11 years old, Halo 3 was released. This is still considered one of the biggest launches of a console/game to date. While I was far too young to be standing outside waiting for a midnight release, I will never forget getting a new console for my birthday with this game.

Finding Myself in Gaming

  1. The first game I beat completely on my own
  2. It wasnt recommended or a big popular title
  3. Just a curious kid figuring out how to get through an underwater dystopia

Why is it my favorite passion?

Gaming has helped me in life in so many ways. Its been an outlet that I can explore in the hardest of times. Not that it doesn't come with its own problems if abused, but it truly has helped shape me into who I am today.

It's a great way to unwind, have fun! Or it can be used a tool for learning. It can even be used to look inward and understand what kind of person you are, or who you want to become!

Learn from the mistakes of the characters you play as, or learn communication skills in team oriented games!

A Way to Keep in Contact

Recently even just moving to St.Louis, it can be hard keeping in contact with friends back in Texas. My friend group decided to give Dungeons and Dragons a shot, and now we play every sunday! Without knowing it, DnD has truly become my life! I look forward to playing every week.
